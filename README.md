# Gabriel Corradini Lourenço Dias

Electrical Engineering undergraduate (micro/nano devices) working at the
interface of fundamental electromagnetic/vacuum physics and engineering.
I work across two tracks, kept separate:

- **Research Assistant, ZPF Technologies — Mechanism-Discrimination Track.**
  The internal falsifiability-and-discrimination discipline for an early-stage
  theoretical framework: testing whether proposed mechanisms are overdetermined,
  whether their scaling laws are consistent, whether they are distinguishable
  from competing mechanisms, and what would falsify each.

  spacecraft-propulsion engineering.
